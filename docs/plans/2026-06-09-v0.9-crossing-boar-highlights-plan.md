## Acceptance scenarios (draft)

1. **Crossing sell** — party with scout+mule, sell mule, gold rises, mule leaves party, success line.
2. **Crossing empty** — no party, step on crossing, lore only, no modal.
3. **Boar volley** — hold boar, first Fight in encounter applies volley only (100% odds shown); second Fight is a normal round.
4. **Exclusion** — hold mule, attempt boar hire, `BOAR_MULE_REFUSED_LINES`, mule highlighted; reverse for boar→mule.
5. **Healer** — mend after fight highlights healer; town enter with healer highlights healer on upkeep.
6. **Farm coverage** — fixed seed, ≥1 farm offers mule and ≥1 offers boar; every farm has food.
